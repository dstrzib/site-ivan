1. Nike

Nike is an American brand founded in 1964. This American Multinational is the king of sports shoes in the world and thus dominates the list of the top 10 sports shoe brands. It designs, develops, manufactures, and markets apparel, accessories, and footwear. Chosen as one of the best sport shoes brands in India, Nike is a delight for athletes and fitness freaks. Nike has offices located in 45 countries across the world. Comfort, flexibility, and durability make the brand’s shoes a hit. From cricket and basketball to running and skateboarding, Nike has a specific pair for each purpose.

2.Puma

Introduced in 1948, Puma is a German multinational company. It sells footwear, apparel, and accessories in the athletic and casual categories. Puma is a pioneer in the industry for creating revolutionary shoes. The company has provided employment to more than 10000 people over 120 countries It was also amongst the first few brands that infused fashion and style into sports. Puma’s sports shoe collection uses futuristic styling, lightweight technology, and bold colors. Running, training, or playing football, you can pick from Puma’s range.

3. Adidas

Born in Germany in 1949, Adidas is the second-largest sportswear company in the world. This top sports shoe brand offers an extensive range of apparel and accessories. The brand serves sports enthusiasts, professional athletes, and the fashion-forward population. Adidas sports shoes are light, long-lasting, and are available in varied colors and prints. These are functional and comfortable while being stylish. Brisk walking on a pleasant morning or hiking in the stormiest weather, a pair from Adidas will always keep you on top of your game.
